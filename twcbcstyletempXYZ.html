Why a Digital Style Guide?

Brand, creative and content guidelines are absolutely essential to large organizations such ours. Consistency and brand messaging can suffer if guidelines arenâ€™t in place. Especially in the digital space, content development is a team-wide effort and must be supported by the right tools and understanding. Our digital audience has unique expectations that shape every piece of content we create. Most importantly, a Digital Style Guide will:

  1. Put our Audiences First.

    Staying in touch with visitor behaviors and activities dictates the language, mediums, and messages we use. Guidelines recognize a link between what our audience needs and the best way to fulfill that need in the most effective way.

  2. Breed Consistency.

    Giving our audience a consistent experience builds loyalty and trust. Guidelines can be a central reference to keep the overall voice/tone consistent, and to ensure creative output reflects the quality of the written content.

  3. Create Better Content.

    A Digital Style Guide is both a practical resource as well as a commitment to well-considered content. Personality, trends, brand values, key messaging, tone, style, image selection and formatting all contribute to the quality of our content â€“ and guidelines keep this all in check.
