Guiding Principles

Content and design on the website should adhere to these principles:

Defer to Content

Give the content center stage in the layout. Design for experiences that are unobtrusive and authentically thoughtful, in order to respect what a visitor needs

Make the Useful Beautiful

Utility satisfies needs, but beauty drives desire. Design for experiences that people love to have. Create aesthetically pleasing offerings that carry a consistent meaning in their form and function.

Design for Flexibility

Patterns and designs are made to be easily adaptable. These guidelines encourage consistency over uniformity -- providing a sense of familiarity and ease with the brand, while still allowing for customization of each content asset.

Inform and Delight

Our visitors are smart. Engage them in moments of inspiration and curiosity. Design for enlightening experiences that encourage critical thinking.

Be Contextually Aware

Be mindful of what users need by providing them the right tools at the right time. Design content for the most desirable outcomes while keeping changing context in mind.
