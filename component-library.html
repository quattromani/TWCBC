            About Component Library

            A byproduct of the Style Guide; a Component Library is a collection of the combined atomic elements that make up the site and become modules and components. Ensuring consistency across the site can be extremely difficult. Component Libraries aim to quell that arduous effort. By providing pre-compiled elements, components, and modules - displaying how they are formed and used provides a benefit to developers who are coming into the project after it has started, or possibly taking over a project as a whole.
